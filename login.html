Welcome to Cape66°

We are a group of business owners who believe in 'paying it forward'. We helped fellow entrepreneurs whenever we could. Helping others allowed us to receive support on our own journeys, start new relationships, & raise awareness of our businesses.

So we're launching an online platform to enable every entrepreneur to do the same and to create a global network of entrepreneurs who believe in 'paying it forward.
