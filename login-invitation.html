Welcome Cape 66°

We started as a group of business owners who believe in 'paying it forward'. We helped fellow entrepreneurs whenever we could. Helping others allowed us to receive support on our own journeys, start new relationships, & raise awareness of our business.

So we launched an online platform to enable entrepreneurs in every country to do the same & to create an international network of entrepreneurs who believe in 'paying it forward'

" From day 1 in Cape 66° an entrepreneur can seek support from the entire network."

In

  • We ask for personal introductions , supplier recommendations , or advice from the entire netowrk. So we receive the support of the members who can open a door or help solve a problem.
  • The platform supports the most helpful entrepreneurs by promoting their business to the entire network - nationally and internationally.
  • The more you help other entrepreneurs , the more exposure your own requests receive and the more support you get.
  • By helping and receiving help , all entrepreneurs have a great way of starting new relationships . You 'll form valuable connections with members you would have never otherwise crossed paths with.
